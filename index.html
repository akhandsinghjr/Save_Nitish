Save Nitish

Story

In the heat of election season, Nitish becomes the most sought-after figure, crucial to every politician's campaign. As rivals plot to capture him for their gain, it's up to you to protect Nitish from being manipulated. Navigate through challenges, dodge traps, and outsmart opponents to ensure Nitish's safety and keep him free!

Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move Nitish around the game area. Collect all the ballot boxes to open the gate and proceed to the next level. Avoid the other politicians at all costs. Press the space bar to shoot slippers and defeat the Politicians. Good luck!
